What it does

A pour is a memory. Keep it — or share it.

Log it

Two taps to remember it forever.

The drink, a photo, a few notes, how it hit — and rank it if you want to. It doesn't have to have alcohol in it: a beer or a cocktail, sure, but a morning coffee, a soda, or a good mocktail all count just the same. If you poured it, Pour keeps it. Logging stays instant and works offline; the memory lasts.

Share it

Private by default. Social when you want.

Add your friends and your pours become a feed you actually want to open — close friends, not followers. See what they're drinking and where, react to a great pour, and keep groups for your regular crew. It's BeReal for the bar, not a public broadcast: you decide, pour by pour, what's just for you and what you share.

Map it

Every pour drops a pin.

Your map fills in as you go — a personal atlas of taprooms, trips, and back porches, free to build and browse. Connect with friends and you'll see their pins too, so you always know where everyone's drinking. Location is opt-in, one pour at a time. Want to show it off? Turn any trip into a designed, shareable map image with a credit.

AI Recaps

Your story, poured back to you.

Turn a week, a month, a whole year — or any stretch you choose — into a warm, written recap of where you went, who you were with, and the pours worth remembering. Keep it to yourself, or share it with the friends who were there. Made the moment you want one.
