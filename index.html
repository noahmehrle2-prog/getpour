What it does

A little ritual after every good pour.

Log it

Two taps to remember it forever.

The drink, a photo, a few notes, how it hit. Beer, wine, a cocktail, or a craft non-alcoholic — if you poured it, Pour keeps it. A calm, private record of everything you drink, with no scores to chase and no feed to perform for.

[ Screenshot: logging a drink ]
Map it

Every pour, pinned to a place.

Add a location to any pour and your map slowly fills in — a personal atlas of taprooms, trips, and back porches. Location is opt-in, one drink at a time.

[ Screenshot: your map ]
Keep it

Your history, only yours.

Search back through everything — by drink, by place, by the night it belonged to. Pour is a journal, not a network. There's no one to follow and no one following you.

[ Screenshot: a saved memory ]
Lifetime Pro

Your year, poured back to you.

Every December, Pour turns your year of pours into a warm, personal recap — the places, the people, the ones worth remembering. A quiet look back at a year well spent.
