How do I log a drink?+
  1. Open Pour and tap the + button.
  2. Add the drink — beer, cocktail, wine, or non-alcoholic — and, if you like, who made it, a photo, and any notes or a rating.
  3. Optionally add who you were with and a location.
  4. Save — it's now in your journal forever.

You can log drinks without an account; that data stays on your device.

How does the map work?+

When you add a location to a drink, Pour drops a pin on your personal map. Over time the map becomes a private history of everywhere you've had a pour.

Location is opt-in and per-entry — Pour only uses your location when you choose to add a place. You can manage location access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

What does Lifetime Pro unlock?+

Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase (not a subscription) that unlocks:

  1. Year in Review — your annual recap, every year.
  2. Advanced map & full history.
  3. Future Pro features, at no extra charge.

The free tier keeps logging your drinks for as long as you like.

How do I restore my purchase?+

If you reinstall Pour or get a new device, you can restore Lifetime Pro at no cost:

  1. Open Pour and go to Profile → Restore Purchases.
  2. Tap Restore Purchases.
  3. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Pro.

Purchases are tied to your Apple ID through the App Store. If a restore doesn't work, email us and we'll help.

Where is my data stored, and is it private?+

Your journal lives on your device. If you sign in, some data also syncs to Pour's private backend to power sync and AI recaps. We don't sell your data or use it for ads. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data or account?+

Deleting the app removes the journal stored on your device. To delete your account and its data from our backend, open Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or email noah.mehrle2@gmail.com and we'll take care of it.

I found a bug or have a feature idea.+

We'd genuinely love to hear it. Email noah.mehrle2@gmail.com with as much detail as you can (and your iOS version helps).
