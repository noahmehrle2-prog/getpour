Memories, not ratings

Looking for an Untappd alternative?

Untappd is great at what it's built for: rating beers, earning badges, and building a public log of what you've had. If that's what you want, keep it — genuinely.

Pour is built for something different. Not "what did you think of this beer?" but "remember this night?" A pour in Pour is the moment around the drink: who you were with, where you were, the weather, the photo, the one-line story. No scores. No badges. No leaderboards. No ads.

Drinking less? That's kind of the point.

Drinking in the U.S. is at a multi-decade low, and most of us are somewhere on the "drink less, but better" curve. A ratings database doesn't have much to say about that. A memory journal does: the Tuesday coffee with your dad counts exactly as much as the barrel-aged stout — because the journal is about the moment, not the ABV.

Bring your history with you

Already have years of check-ins? Export your data from Untappd (Settings → Export), then in Pour: Social → My Profile → Import from Untappd or CSV. Your history lands on your map, in your stats, and in your first year-in-review — as memories this time. Any generic CSV with a name and date column works too.
